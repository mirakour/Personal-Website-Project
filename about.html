Who Am I?

When I'm not coding, I'm exploring my passions.





Fitness Enthusiast


Staying active isn't just a routine—it's a lifestyle. Whether it’s weight training, yoga, or hitting the pavement for a run, I believe movement fuels both the body and the mind. Fitness keeps me disciplined, energized, and ready to tackle any challenge, both in and out of work.



Self Made Chef


Food is more than just fuel—it’s creativity on a plate. I love experimenting with flavors, perfecting my signature dishes, and making healthy meals that don’t compromise on taste. Cooking is my way of unwinding, exploring cultures, and sharing joy with others.



Furr Mom


My life wouldn’t be complete without my four-legged best friend, Birkin. Being a pet parent has taught me patience, unconditional love, and how to always make time for play. Whether it’s outdoor adventures or cozy nights in with him, Birkin is my daily dose of happiness.
